About Us

Sri Balaji Medical is dedicated to serving our community with a comprehensive range of healthcare products and services. We prioritize your well-being, offering everything from essential prescription medications to specialized medical devices and expert consultations. Our commitment to quality and patient care ensures that you receive trusted products and professional guidance every time you visit.

Our Products & Services

Prescription Medicines

We stock a wide variety of prescription medications. Our pharmacists ensure safe and accurate dispensing, and are available to answer your questions.

Medical Devices

Explore our selection of high-quality medical devices for home use and professional needs. From blood pressure monitors to glucose meters, we stock reliable equipment to support your health management.

Lab Test Services

We facilitate various lab tests with trusted partners, ensuring accurate and timely results for your diagnostic needs. Please contact us for more information on available tests and booking procedures.

Wellness Products

Enhance your well-being with our range of health and wellness products, including nutritional supplements, personal care items, and more.

Other Products & Services
